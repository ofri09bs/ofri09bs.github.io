## Low-Level Exploitation
* **Stack Pivoting:** I learned how you can control the program workflow and **"transfer" the stack** into another place where you have control on it. 
In the challenge "simplelogin" using only 4 bytes overflow I was able to **overwrite ebp** and this caused the **stack to jump to a global varibale** I controled. You can check out the full writeup I made in my blog.  
* **Address Leaking:** I learned how to **overcome the ASLR** protection by **leaking address** of Libc functions in run-time and then calculatig the **Libc base address**, and from here because
the offsets are always the same in Libc, you can get any function address and then preform multipule **GOT overwrites**.
